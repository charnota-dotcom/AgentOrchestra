# 🚀 AgentOrchestra: Your Robot Friend Guide!

Welcome to AgentOrchestra! This is a special tool that lets you build and talk to smart "Robot Friends" (we call them **Agents** and **Drones**). Here is how you can get started!

---

## 1. The Big Idea: Plans vs. Robots

Imagine you want to build a LEGO set.
- A **Blueprint** is the instruction booklet. it tells you what the robot should be like, what its "Brain" is, and what special skills it has.
- A **Drone** or **Agent** is the actual robot you built using those instructions!

## 2. Meet the Robots

There are two kinds of robots you can make:

### 🚁 Drones (The Helpers)
Drones are robots that live in your web browser. They need your help to talk! You copy what they say, paste it into a website, and then bring the answer back. They are great for simple tasks where you want to watch every step.

### 🤖 Agents (The Auto-Robots)
Agents are super smart! They run on your computer all by themselves. You give them a job, and they just do it! They can read files, write code, and solve hard puzzles without you needing to copy and paste anything.

## 3. How to Set Them Up

### Step 1: Make a Plan (Blueprints)
Go to the **Blueprints** tab.
- Click **"+ Drone"** if you want a browser helper who needs your help to talk.
- Click **"+ Agent"** if you want a smart, autonomous robot who works by himself.

**Filling out your plan:**
- Give your robot a name.
- Pick a **Brain** (Provider and Model). For Agents, you'll pick from smart computer brains. For Drones, it's locked to your browser.
- Pick a **Role**. Is it a "Worker" who just does jobs, or a "Supervisor" who tells other robots what to do?
- **Skills (Superpowers):** For Agents, click the **"Browse..."** button to open a window full of special powers. You can't type these in; you have to pick them from your library!
- **Chat URL:** For Drones, this tells the robot which website to open for you.

**Changing your mind:**
- If you made a Drone but want it to be an Agent later, select it in the list and click **"Convert to Agent…"**. This is like upgrading your robot's brain so it can work all by itself!

### Step 2: Manage Superpowers (Skills)
Go to the **Skills** tab. This is where you can see all the special powers your robots can have.
- You can create your own superpowers by clicking "+ New".
- Give the power a name and write down exactly what the robot should do when using that power.
- You can change or delete powers here too.
- When you make a robot (Step 1), you can pick these superpowers from the list!

### Step 3: Start the Robot (Deploy)
Go to the **Drones** or **Agents** tab. Click the "Deploy" button.
- Pick one of your Plans (Blueprints).
- Click "Deploy", and your robot is alive!

### Step 3: Let Robots Talk (Flights)
If you want robots to work together, you can link them!
- On the **Canvas**, draw a line between two robots.
- Or, click "Edit References" on a robot to tell it which other robots it should listen to.
- When they are linked, they share their "Memory" so they don't forget what the other robot already did.

## 4. How They Work

When you send a message, the "Brain" you picked starts thinking.
- If it's an **Agent**, you'll see the words appearing one-by-one as it thinks (just like someone typing!).
- If it's a **Drone**, it will give you a special message to copy and paste.

That's it! You are now a Robot Commander. Go build something cool! 🚀
